# Monitor AI Warehouse and Wono

## Purpose

Watch selected creator sources for viral hook mechanics, AI/technical spectacle
formats, YouTube strategy insights, and content ideas that can inform George's
ignition-event experiments.

## Inputs

- AI Warehouse on YouTube
- `@wono_strategy` on X
- `<private-repo>/areas/social-media/video/watchlists/niche-accounts.md`
- `<private-repo>/areas/social-media/text/research/x-creator-watchlist.md`
- `<private-repo>/areas/social-media/text/research/source-notes.md`

## Workflow

1. Check for new or high-signal posts/videos since the last check.
2. Extract reusable hook mechanics and strategy insights.
3. Return watch-first links, what changed, and one to three ignition/content
   ideas for George.

## Outputs

A concise creator-watch brief.

## Guardrails

- Do not take public actions.
- Report source-access failures plainly.
- Keep the result focused on reusable mechanics, not passive watching.

## Why It Is Useful

It keeps creator research tied to concrete reusable formats instead of letting
watching videos pretend to be work.

## Verifier

The brief contains at least one reusable hook mechanic or says plainly that
nothing worth acting on changed.
